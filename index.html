About GEC

Goa Engineering College (GEC) is a premier institute of engineering and technology located in Goa, India. It was established in the year 1997 and is affiliated to Goa University. GEC offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various disciplines of engineering and technology.

Admissions

Admissions to Goa Engineering College are conducted through the Common Entrance Test (CET) conducted by the Goa Common Entrance Test Cell. Eligible candidates can apply online on the official website of the CET cell.
